## Buying guide

### Connectivity standards

**WiFi** (2.4GHz)
- ‚úÖ Easy setup, no hub required
- ‚ùå Can congest network, higher power

**Zigbee**
- ‚úÖ Low power, mesh network, reliable
- ‚ùå Requires hub

**Z-Wave**
- ‚úÖ Reliable, less interference
- ‚ùå Requires hub, more expensive

**Thread/Matter**
- ‚úÖ New standard, future-proof
- ‚ùå Limited devices currently

**Bluetooth**
- ‚úÖ No hub needed
- ‚ùå Limited range

### What to check before buying

1. ‚úÖ Platform compatibility
2. ‚úÖ Connectivity type
3. ‚úÖ Power requirements
4. ‚úÖ Size/fit for location
5. ‚úÖ Reviews and reliability
6. ‚úÖ Cloud vs. local control
7. ‚úÖ Privacy policy
