# Privacy policy

**Last updated:** {{ site.time | date: '%B %d, %Y' }}

Home Automation Cookbook is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

## Information we collect

### Website usage data

We may collect basic analytics data to understand how visitors use our site:

- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Referral sources
- Device type and browser

This data is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally.

### No personal data collection

Home Automation Cookbook does not:

- Require account registration
- Collect email addresses (unless you contact us directly)
- Store personal information in databases
- Use cookies for tracking across websites
- Sell or share data with third parties

## GitHub contributions

If you contribute to this project on GitHub:

- Your GitHub username and contributions are publicly visible
- GitHub's privacy policy applies to your use of their platform
- You can review GitHub's privacy policy at [github.com/privacy](https://github.com/site/privacy)

## Third-party links

Our site contains links to external resources and products. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

## External platforms mentioned

The automation recipes reference various smart home platforms (Home Assistant, SmartThings, Google Home, Apple HomeKit, Amazon Alexa, Hubitat). Each platform has its own privacy policy that applies when you use their services.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/madskristensen/HomeAutomationCookbook/issues).

## Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
