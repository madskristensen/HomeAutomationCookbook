# Entertainment automations

Music and light share a natural kinship. A film begins and the room dims. Shower water runs and a favorite playlist fills the steam. The best entertainment automations feel less like technology and more like a house that understands the mood you are reaching for - and meets you there without being asked.

<div class="category-intro" markdown="1">

## Why entertainment automation?

**Soundtrack your life** - Music starts automatically for different activities

**Perfect ambiance** - Lights dim automatically when watching movies

**Morning motivation** - Shower music plays without touching your phone

**Welcome home** - Arrive to music and perfect lighting

**Physical control** - Buttons and switches are faster than voice commands

**Consistent volume** - No more blasting speakers or inaudible audio

</div>

## Best practices

<div class="placement-grid">

<div class="placement-card">
<h3>Speaker placement</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Kitchen:</strong> Above cabinets or on counter</li>
<li><strong>Bathroom:</strong> Away from direct water (IP rating)</li>
<li><strong>Living room:</strong> Multiple speakers for stereo</li>
<li><strong>Outdoor:</strong> Weather-resistant models</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="placement-card">
<h3>Network reliability</h3>
<ul>
<li>Use 2.4GHz WiFi (better range)</li>
<li>Wired ethernet when possible</li>
<li>Keep speakers away from microwave</li>
<li>Update firmware regularly</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="placement-card">
<h3>Volume strategy</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Morning:</strong> Low 20-30%</li>
<li><strong>Daytime:</strong> Medium 40-50%</li>
<li><strong>Late night:</strong> Low 15-25%</li>
<li>Bedroom always quieter</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="placement-card">
<h3>Daily reset at 4 AM</h3>
<ul>
<li>Reset volume levels</li>
<li>Dissolve temporary speaker groups</li>
<li>Stop any playing music</li>
<li>Ready for morning routines</li>
</ul>
</div>

## Troubleshooting

<div class="troubleshooting-grid">

<div class="issue-card">
<div class="issue-header">
<h3>Music not starting automatically</h3>
</div>
<div class="issue-solutions">
<ul>
<li>Verify trigger (light, door, presence)</li>
<li>Check music service is linked</li>
<li>Ensure speaker is powered on</li>
<li>Check automation is enabled</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<div class="issue-card">
<div class="issue-header">
<h3>Lights not dimming for TV</h3>
</div>
<div class="issue-solutions">
<ul>
<li>Verify TV power monitoring detects on state</li>
<li>Check threshold is correct (usually 50W+)</li>
<li>Test lights respond to manual commands</li>
<li>Ensure automation time conditions are met</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<div class="issue-card">
<div class="issue-header">
<h3>Volume too loud or quiet</h3>
</div>
<div class="issue-solutions">
<ul>
<li>Check last manual volume change</li>
<li>Verify daily reset automation runs</li>
<li>Adjust preset volume levels</li>
<li>Check speaker-specific settings</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<div class="issue-card">
<div class="issue-header">
<h3>Shower music stops too soon</h3>
</div>
<div class="issue-solutions">
<ul>
<li>Increase timer duration</li>
<li>Use "door closed" instead of "lights off"</li>
<li>Add delay before stopping</li>
<li>Check for conflicting automations</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
